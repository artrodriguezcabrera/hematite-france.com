About Us

HEMATITE is a French distribution company, that specializes in exporting luxury brands to Sub-Saharan Africa. HEMATITE is a distributor of world-renowned brands in the luggage, leather goods, jewelry, watches, perfumes, and fashion accessories sectors. Our business philosophy is to provide qualitative and selective work with the brands to maximize sell-out as well as to respect the brand image. Thanks to our knowledge of the African market specificities, we can offer a dedicated service in terms of logistics and marketing.

Our Team

Our team has extensive experience in the luxury sector and selective distribution. It is made up of experts in exports to Sub-Saharan Africa.

Simon Augué

Simon AUGUÉ, manager and founder of HEMATITE, has worked in the luxury industry since 2001 and developed the GIVENCHY / KENZO / FENDI perfume brands of the LVMH group in African markets from 2007 to 2012. Since 2012, thanks to this experience, he has successfully deployed the brands entrusted to us.
